Common questions

Can't find what you need? WhatsApp us — we usually reply within the hour.

How big are the groups?

We cap groups so every learner gets airtime — typically 6 to 12 students per session. Smaller for matric exam clinics.

Are the sessions online, in person, or both?

Both. Most weekly classes run live online via video call, with in-person Saturday clinics during exam terms in Sun Valley. Online learners get the same materials and recording access.

What if my child joins mid-term?

No problem. We'll catch them up with the previous weeks' recordings and past paper packs, and the tutor will check in personally to identify gaps.

How do I pay, and is there a contract?

EFT to our Capitec account (details in the footer), using the learner's email as the reference. No contracts — you pay month by month.

Do you cover engineering / first-year university maths?

Yes, on request and outside the standard group timetable. Get in touch and we'll arrange a tailored session schedule.

What if I'm not in South Africa?

As long as you can join a live session in SAST (UTC+2) or watch the recording within a reasonable window, you can enrol from anywhere. Fees are quoted in ZAR.

Subject 01 — Curriculum

Mathematics curriculum

Grade 10 · Foundations

Building the toolkit

  • Algebraic expressions, products, factorisation
  • Equations & inequalities — quadratic, simultaneous
  • Functions & graphs — linear, quadratic, hyperbola, exponential
  • Trigonometry — ratios, special angles, 2D triangles
  • Analytical geometry — distance, midpoint, gradient
  • Financial maths — simple & compound interest
  • Statistics & probability basics

Grade 11 · Building Depth

Quadratics & Geometry

  • Exponents, surds, rationalising
  • Quadratic formula, nature of roots
  • Trig identities, reduction formulae, compound angles
  • Trig equations & graphs
  • Euclidean geometry — circle theorems & proofs
  • Analytical geometry — circle equation, inclination
  • Counting & probability

Grade 12 · Matric Prep

Calculus & Series

  • Sequences & series — arithmetic, geometric, sigma
  • Functions — inverses, logarithms, exponentials
  • Calculus — first principles, differentiation, cubic sketching, optimisation
  • 3D trigonometry, compound & double angles
  • Tangents to circles, analytical geometry
  • Financial maths — future/present value, loan repayments
  • Statistics — regression, correlation, normal distribution
  • Full past paper analysis & exam strategy

Subject 02 — Curriculum

Physical Sciences curriculum

Grade 10 · Foundations

Matter, Motion & Reactions

  • Mechanics — vectors in 1D, equations of motion
  • Energy — gravitational PE, kinetic energy, conservation
  • Waves, sound & light
  • Electricity — current, potential difference, Ohm's Law
  • Matter & materials — atomic theory, periodic table, bonding
  • Chemical change — reactions, balancing equations

Grade 11 · Building Depth

Newton, Electrostatics & Chemistry

  • Vectors in 2D — resultant forces, equilibrium
  • Newton's laws — friction, inclined planes
  • Electrostatics — Coulomb's law, electric field
  • Circuits — series & parallel, internal resistance
  • Refraction, diffraction, Snell's law
  • Stoichiometry, mole concept, % yield
  • Ideal gases — gas laws

Grade 12 · Matric Prep

Momentum, Organic Chem & Exam Prep

  • Momentum & impulse — conservation, Newton's 3rd law
  • Vertical projectile motion
  • Work, energy & power — work-energy theorem
  • Doppler effect — source & observer movement
  • Electrodynamics — AC/DC generators, transformers
  • Photoelectric effect — photon model, work function
  • Organic chemistry — naming, functional groups, reactions
  • Chemical equilibrium — Le Chatelier's, Kc
  • Acids, bases & pH; electrochemistry
  • Full past paper analysis & exam strategy
